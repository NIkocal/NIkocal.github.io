Box Model

Whether your building a simple static site or a complex blog, when designing your project you will have to understand how the CSS Box Model interacts with elements on the page. According to the Box Model every element on a page is surrounded by a rectangular box. The Box model has layers like an onion, at the center is the content, then follows the padding, border and the margin is at the the outer region.

The padding surrounds the contents and is used to create space within the element.It's calculated by the distance between the elements border and the content.

The border is between the padding and margin. They can be used to give content a unique frame or even serve an easy way to organize the layout of the page.

While the padding is used to define the whitespace within the box, the margin is responsible for the whitespace outside of te box. It creates space for the box by interacting with with elements on the page.
